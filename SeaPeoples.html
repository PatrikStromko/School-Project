Who were Sea Peoples?

- Sea Peoples were seaborne and land invaders who troubled the Mediterranean and Egypt

- No one knows for certain who these people were and where they come from

- There is a theory that Sea Peoples were responsible for collapse of Late Bronze Age civilizations

Historical evidence of Sea Peoples

- There have been many attempts to determine the origins of Sea Peoples

- Majority of evidence come from the Egyptian hieroglyphs and other historical sources

- One Egytian inscription proclaims:"They came from the sea in their warships and none could stand against them".

Devastating the Ancient World

- Witnesses left behind inscriptions about the terrors Sea Peoples inflicted

- Egyptian Pharaoh Ramesses III. wrote:"They desolated its people and its land was like that which has never happened".

- After destroying neighboring Egypt lands, after 200 years Sea Peoples continued their raids throughout the Mediterranean

- The last recorded raid of Sea People wasn't successful - Egyptian army destroyed their fleet during the Battle of the Delta
